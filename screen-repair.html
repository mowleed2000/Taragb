What's Involved

A damaged screen isn't just an eyesore — it affects everything you do on your phone. We replace cracked, broken and unresponsive screens across all major brands including iPhone, Samsung, Google Pixel and more. We use premium quality display parts that restore full touch sensitivity, brightness and colour accuracy. Most screen repairs are completed within the hour.

Signs You Need This Repair

  • Cracked or shattered glass panel
  • Black, bleeding or flickering screen display
  • Touch screen unresponsive to taps or gestures
  • Horizontal lines or dead pixels showing on display
